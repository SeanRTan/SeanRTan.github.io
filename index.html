Introduction

An aspiring Game Developer and AI programmer. Experienced in Python, currently learning C# and C++, along with Unity 5.


Staffordshire University

I am currently enrolled in Staffordshire University as a first year under their BSc (Hons) Games Programming course. My eventual goal is to leave Staffordshire as a specialist in Artificial Intelligence and to go on to work for indie and AAA companies.


Passion

Despite knowing from a young age that I wanted to program and make games for people to enjoy, it was only recently that my passion evolved into becoming an Artificial Intelligence programmer.

The main reason for this was due to games with AI considered complex like Doom Eternal, Ready or Not and Ultrakill.

I found the AI in these games intriguing and extremely enjoyable to watch and interact with as they would react to the player's actions accordingly, yet would also take initiative on some actions despite the lack of player intervention.

This type of AI is seomthing that I would like to emulate in one of my games one day, as to me, the most important things about an AI is that they must be fun to interact with and that they get the player thinking.

Portfolio

Python Projects

SuperGun

SuperGun was a program made for my Computer Science A-Level coursework. It is a Top-Down shooter inspired by Hotline Miami and the original Doom, it also includes a login system utilising a local SQL database.

It has an inventory of 3 different weapons for the player, 3 maps that are randomly chosen, 2 different enemy types, a health system with med kits and a leaderboard system connected to the login database

Unity 5 Projects

Sink the Sacrifice

Sink the Sacrifice is an on-the-rails down scrolling shooter for one of my University modules, completed in just over a week.

It has 4 Levels and a tutorial level that quickly introduces the player to the base mechanics of the game, such as rocking the platform left and right, launching their knives, their Health Bar and a unique "Doom Meter", that when filled completely, will send the player to the final boss instantaneously.

Footage of the game can be seen on my YouTube channel or by clicking on the Sink the Sacrifice image.

Marble Madness

Marble Madness is a framework that we were given by one of my modules to work on. Our task with Marble Madness was to make at least one extra level and to make a new placeable.

I decided to make a simple cannon that shoots cannonballs periodically to knock the player off their initial course. The developmental footage of which can be seen on my YouTube channel or by clicking on the Marble Madness image

Additionally I made 2 extra levels my first level being one that uses a lot of unused premade placeables along with my cannon, and the other being based off a pachinko machine and completely leaves it up to chance due to the amount obstacles that change your path
