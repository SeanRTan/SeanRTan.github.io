AI Decision-Making & Pathfinding

Created using Unity 5, the project demonstrates the fundamanetals of AI decision-making and pathfinding within separate scenes. The techniques learnt all culminate in the final scene, where an AI survives against a horde of enemies.

The AI has access to multiple weapons while health and ammo packs randomly spawn around the map.

John Wick: Neon

Created using Unity 5, John Wick: Neon is a top-down shooter heaily inspired by the John Wick movies and Hotline Miami.

The player has access to a pistol, a rifle and a shotgun and there are 3 levels plus a tutorial to play. The project was my first experience in AI pathfinding and decision-making, a plugin was downloaded to control all the enemy movement.
