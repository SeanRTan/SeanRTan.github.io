Introduction

An aspiring Game Developer and AI programmer. Experienced in Python, currently learning C# and C++, along with Unity 5.

Staffordshire University

I am currently enrolled in Staffordshire University as a first year under their BSc (Hons) Games Programming course. My eventual goal is to leave Staffordshire as a specialist in Artificial Intelligence and to go on to work for indie and AAA companies.

Passion

Despite knowing from a young age that I wanted to program and make games for people to enjoy, it was only recently that my passion evolved into becoming an Artificial Intelligence programmer.

The main reason for this was due to games with AI considered complex like Doom Eternal, Ready or Not and Ultrakill.

I found the AI in these games intriguing and extremely enjoyable to watch and interact with as they would react to the player's actions accordingly, yet would also take initiative on some actions despite the lack of player intervention.

This type of AI is seomthing that I would like to emulate in one of my games one day, as to me, the most important things about an AI is that they must be fun to interact with and that they get the player thinking.

Portfolio

Unreal Engine Projects

UF-Foe

To view the trailer, you can click on the UF-Foe Poster.

UF-Foe is a local co-op multiplayer horde shooter built by me and a team of others for our Collab project. In the game you could play as a class from a selection of 4 options where each class had a unique weapon. The goal of the game is to collect all your ship parts scattered across the map to fix it and escape

My contribution to UF-Foe was the creation of all the enemy AI, which consists of a melee enemy, which had 2 variations made from it, a ranged shooting enemy and a boss style enemy that has a second phase where they charge at the players.

UF-Foe was made in about 2 months.

Unity 5 Projects

John Wick Neon

To view a demo of the completed game, you can click on the John Wick Neon menu screen.

John Wick Neon is a top-down shooter heavily inspired by the John Wick franchise and the Hotline Miami games. It was created as the 4th project for my Rapid Games Prototyping university module. It features an ammo system, sprite sheet animations, a weapon inventory, two enemy types and enemy AI pathfinding.

John Wick Neon was made in just over 2 weeks.

Cosmonaut Hustle

To view a demo of the completed game, you can click on the Cosmonaut Hustle menu screen.

Cosmonaut Hustle is a 2D platformer centered around rocket jumping and has influences from the games DOOM and Papers, Please. It was created as the 3rd project for my Rapid Games Prototyping university module. It features sprite animations, 2 distinct enemy types, cutscenes and a rocket jumping mechanic that took me 4 iterations of modifying the physics and the character controller to get to a satisfactory level.

Cosmonaut hustle was made in just over 3 weeks

Sink the Sacrifice

To view a demo of the completed game, you can click on the Sink the Sacrifice menu screen.

Sink the Sacrifice is an on-the-rails down scrolling shooter for one of my University modules. It is an on-the-rails down scrolling shooter inspired by DownWell featuring 5 levels, a variety of enemy types, a physics based movement system where you would sway the platform to avoid obstacles and a unique "Doom" meter. Doom meter fills up via enemy collisions, filling it up entirely will bring you to the final boss.

Sink the Sacrifice was made in just over a week.
